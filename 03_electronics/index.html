Weekly Overview

Circuit Simulation with Tinkercad and the Falstad applet.
Gathering tools and components. Safety discussion.

  • Microcontroller
  • Breadboard

  • Use a breadboard.
  • Build an LED circuit.

  • Calculate Power and discuss energy.
5:00-5:15: Break. Install Arduino IDE.
5:15-5:30: Demonstration of Metro M0 board and Blink.
5:30 - 6:00: Breakout rooms: get Metro M0 installed, run the Blink program.
  • Blink with built-in LED
  • Blink with an LED on the breadboard.
  • Microcontroller as a voltmeter: ADC (Analog to Digital Converter, or "Analog in") and Serial.

Lab Sections

Continue the Intro to electronics.
Explore other components: Capacitors, diodes, sensors. Continue to work with the Microcontroller ADC.

Assignment:

  • Use the Microcontroller Analog in (ADC) to measure a voltage.
  • Make a circuit that does something (motion or light). Use a multimeter to measure the behavior of your circuit.
  • Simulate your circuit.
  • Document your work and learning.
