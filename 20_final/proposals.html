Proposals

Description:

Due Monday, 2/26 11:59pm! For this stage of the Final Project, you should work with your partners to develop three distinct proposals for your group's Final Project. This can include any combination of your individual ideas, but should be further developed to answer the following questions:

  • What is your research question?
  • What are you seeking to discover?
  • What is your metric for success?

Remember that you have multiple team members and over a month to work on this project. Make sure your proposals are appropriately ambitious. Aim high -- it's a lot easier to scale down an over-ambitious idea than to scale up an underwhelming one. You should write a paragraph for each proposal, ordered by preference (put your collective favorite idea first). Proposals should be distinct (not just variations of each other). You may include images or references if it helps to convey your ideas.

Submission:

Post your text to a new thread on the forum, with the first names of the group members as the subject heading by Monday, 2/26 11:59pm.
