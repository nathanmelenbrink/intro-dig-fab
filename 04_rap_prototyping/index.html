Agenda:

5:00: Breakout rooms: Use the L9110 H Bridge Power Driver to drive a motor. Write a new function to give the motor commands.
5:45: Quick prototypes and mechanical design.

Assignment:

Make and document either (a) a kinetic sculpture or (b) a quick prototype of a possible final project. This should integrate an electronic circuit with physical components. In addition to components in the kit, feel free to use materials you find around the house (cardboard, toothpicks, coat hangers, tape and glue, etc.).

Sean's Spirograph

A light-actuated drawing machine
Very quick prototypes
