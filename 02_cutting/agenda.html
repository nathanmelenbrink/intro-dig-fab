Assignment:

Work through a Fusion 360 tutorial, like this or some of these. (or other if you are using other software) and document your learning.

Select at least 2 components from the kit (or other simple household objects). Measure them using calipers, and model them in Fusion 360 (or other program). Create an assembly of multiple components, and document your work on your webpage. You may also incorporate items from our existing library of kit parts. Contributions of kit items that are not already modeled are welcome.
